Adam Savage said:

"Humans do two things that make us unique from all other animals; we use tools and tell stories. And when you make something, you’re doing both at once."
That is a beautiful feeling. We create magic in every school lab, makerspace, and workshop we’ve visited. We love experiencing stories as oral tales, pictures and videos, or even in the form of tutorials. Here there is the start point for you to create your lab stories.
